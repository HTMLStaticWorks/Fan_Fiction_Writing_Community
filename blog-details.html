How to Build Tension in Your Chapters

By Admin • June 27, 2026

Tension is the lifeblood of any good story. In fan fiction, keeping your readers on the edge of their seats between chapters is essential.

Here are some key strategies:

1. Raise the Stakes

Ensure that with every chapter, the consequences of failure become greater. If your characters lose, what happens?

2. The Cliffhanger

Don't resolve the primary conflict at the end of a chapter. Leave a lingering question.

3. Emotional Conflict

Action is great, but emotional tension between beloved characters often holds more weight for readers.
