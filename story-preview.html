Chapter 1: The Silence

  • Chapter 1
  • Chapter 2

The stars were always silent, but today, that silence felt heavy. Tony adjusted the monitors, the blue light reflecting off his tired eyes. It had been forty-eight hours since the last transmission from Earth.

"Anything?" Peter asked, floating into the command module. His voice lacked its usual energetic bounce.

"Nothing but static, kid," Tony replied, leaning back and rubbing his temples. "It's like someone threw a blanket over the entire comms array."

The ship groaned around them, a subtle vibration that reminded them how fragile their metal bubble was in the vastness of the Andromeda galaxy. They were supposed to be mapping a new nebula, not playing hide and seek with the rest of humanity.

Suddenly, the proximity alarm blared, a sharp, piercing sound that shattered the quiet. A massive shadow eclipsed the main viewing port.

"Mr. Stark..." Peter whispered, staring out into the void. "I don't think we're alone anymore."
